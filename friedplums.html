• Fried Plums is something between a collected sketchbook, comic, and poetry zine.
    Collecting a series of touchpad illustrations and one-panel comics that I created during
    the summer and autumn of 2016, Fried Plums explores relationships women have
    between their own selves and each other, as well as
    some of my personal introspection.

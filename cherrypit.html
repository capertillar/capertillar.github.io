• Cherry Pit is an upcoming interactive comic set to debut by August 2018. Inspired
    by Choose Your Own Adventure stories, the reader is able to make
    a decision in almost every scene with a variety of endings and paths. Cherry Pit follows the journey of
    a young woman obsessed with finding and being killed by her idol, Murder Puppy, a celebrity who
    is famous for her public executions.


    (Sample page. Not a final version.)

    Exploring themes of self-hatred and self-harm, celebrity idolization, and unhealthy relationships,
    Cherry Pit is a violent and gleeful adventure through a fantastical world that loves
    itself a charming killer.

    Full color. ~70 pages.
    Digital and physical distribution to come.
